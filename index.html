What is a QR code?
A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of barcode that can store information such as URLs, text, contact details, or payment data. It can be scanned using a smartphone camera or QR code reader.
What can I use QR codes for?
QR codes are commonly used for sharing website links, promoting events, enabling contactless payments, providing product information, and connecting users to apps or social media.
Do I need an app to scan QR codes?
Most modern smartphones can scan QR codes directly with their built-in camera. Some older devices may require a QR code scanning app.
Is there a limit to the amount of information I can store in a QR code?
Yes, but, for best results, keep the content short (like a URL).
Can I print QR codes on any material?
Yes, QR codes can be printed on paper, packaging, posters, or even fabric. Just ensure the size and contrast are sufficient for scanning.
